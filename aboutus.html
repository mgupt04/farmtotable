Why We Do What We Do

Food at The Garden Plate is more than just normal food. It brings you closer to the nature and beauty of the world. We started with the simple objective of serving fresh and delicious vegetarian food that is sustainable. Come along on a journey where every ingredient has a purpose and every dish has a story.

I founded The Garden Plate for a reason: so there would be a place for people to enjoy great vegetarian food, knowing with every food they eat, they support local farmers and take care of the planet. Every single dish on our menu reflects freshness, honest ingredients and sustainability. We cook using only electricity. Thank you for being part of our journey.


Come, Join us on the journey for a sustainable enviornment!
