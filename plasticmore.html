Plastic Free

As part of our dedication to reducing environmental harm, we are committed to eliminating single-use plastics from our operations. We have replaced traditional plastic items such as straws, utensils, and takeout containers with eco-friendly alternatives made from biodegradable materials like bamboo, cornstarch, and recycled paper. Additionally, we encourage customers to bring their own reusable containers and cups, offering incentives such as discounts to promote this practice. By phasing out plastics and embracing sustainable materials, we aim to significantly reduce waste and contribute to a cleaner, healthier planet.
